1. Model Risk Management (MRM)

Models are the backbone of modern finance, but they also introduce inherent risks. Our team delivers end-to-end support across the full model lifecycle to help financial institutions establish, operate, and enhance effective model risk frameworks, transforming potential vulnerabilities into controlled opportunities.

Our Core MRM Capabilities:

  • Framework Design & Implementation: Developing and embedding robust Model Risk Management Frameworks (MRMF) tailored to your organizational structure and risk appetite. This includes defining policies, procedures, and governance structures.
  • Enterprise-Wide Model Inventories: Creating and maintaining comprehensive, dynamic inventories of all models in use across your enterprise, ensuring transparency and control.
  • Model Tiering & Materiality Assessments: Systematically classifying models based on their impact and complexity to prioritize risk management efforts and allocate resources efficiently.
  • Documentation & Governance: Establishing rigorous standards for model documentation, version control, and robust model change governance to ensure audit readiness and operational integrity.
  • Validation Policy & Procedure Development: Crafting clear, actionable Model Validation Policies and Procedures that align with best practices and regulatory expectations.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring your MRM framework is fully compliant with stringent regulatory expectations, including SR 11-7, ECB TRIM, and PRA SS1/23.

AI Enablement: We integrate advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML)-driven tools to automate and streamline core MRM processes. This includes intelligent automation of model documentation review, precise risk classification, and proactive alert generation from validation reports, significantly enhancing efficiency and accuracy.

2. Model Validation & Independent Review

Independent validation is paramount for model credibility and regulatory assurance. We provide highly rigorous model validations designed not only to meet internal governance standards but also to exceed external regulatory expectations, giving you peace of mind and demonstrating due diligence.

Our Independent Review & Validation Process:

  • Comprehensive Model Validation: Conducting independent validation of a broad spectrum of models, including those for credit, market, liquidity, Asset-Liability Management (ALM), and insurance (e.g., Solvency II).
  • Back-testing, Benchmarking & Challenge: Performing in-depth back-testing against historical data, rigorous benchmarking against industry standards, and constructive challenge of model assumptions, methodologies, and outcomes.
  • AI/ML Model Review: Specializing in the validation of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning models, incorporating advanced explainability analysis using techniques like SHAP, LIME, and Partial Dependence Plots to ensure transparency and interpretability.
  • Implementation & Data Integrity Assessment: Thoroughly assessing the accuracy and integrity of model implementation and the underlying data used in the model development and execution.
  • Validation Artifacts & Remediation: Developing clear, concise validation reports, comprehensive findings logs, and actionable remediation plans to address identified deficiencies effectively.

AI Enablement: We leverage automated testing frameworks for rapid and repeatable validation processes, complemented by generative AI tools for producing high-quality validation commentary. Furthermore, our anomaly detection algorithms provide early warnings on subtle shifts in model performance, enabling proactive intervention.

In an ever-evolving regulatory landscape, staying ahead is crucial. ModeLIQ decodes and strategically aligns complex global regulatory requirements with your entire model and risk architecture. Key Regulatory & Compliance Areas:

AI Enablement: We utilize AI-driven compliance trackers that continuously monitor regulatory updates, automatically mapping new requirements to your existing frameworks. Our regulation summarization tools provide concise, actionable intelligence, supporting dynamic regulatory mapping and efficient change management within your organization.

4. AI & Machine Learning Model Risk

While AI/ML offers unparalleled efficiency and sophistication, it also introduces unique challenges related to opacity, bias, and compliance risk. ModeLIQ ensures that your adoption of AI is responsibly governed, explainable, and trustworthy, allowing you to harness its power without compromising integrity or regulatory standing.

Our Specialized AI/ML Model Risk Services:

  • Model Design & Lifecycle Governance: Establishing robust governance frameworks for the entire lifecycle of AI/ML models, from conception and development through deployment, monitoring, and retirement.
  • Bias Testing & Fairness Metrics: Implementing rigorous bias testing and utilizing advanced fairness metrics to identify and mitigate unintended biases within AI models, ensuring equitable outcomes.
  • Adverse Impact Assessments: Conducting comprehensive assessments to identify and address potential adverse impacts of AI models on various stakeholders or demographic groups.
  • Responsible AI Policies & Documentation: Developing clear, actionable Responsible AI policies and ensuring thorough documentation that demonstrates adherence to ethical principles and regulatory expectations.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Navigating the emerging regulatory landscape for AI, including compliance with the EU AI Act and responding to evolving guidelines from prudential authorities like the PRA's AI Discussion Papers.
  • Explainability Tools & Implementation: Implementing and leveraging advanced explainability tools such as SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations), LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations), Partial Dependence Plots (PDP), Counterfactuals, and Anchors to provide clear insights into AI model behavior.

AI Enablement: We don't just enable AI; we govern it with precision. Our internal proprietary tools are designed to assess explainability, bias, and robustness at scale across your entire AI/ML model estate, providing continuous oversight and assurance.

Anticipate tail risk and navigate regulatory scrutiny with confidence. ModeLIQ designs and implements robust stress testing frameworks powered by a judicious blend of expert judgment and sophisticated AI modeling. Our approaches equip you to understand potential vulnerabilities and proactively manage capital and liquidity risks.

Our Stress Testing & Scenario Design Expertise:

  • AI-Generated Scenarios: Leveraging advanced AI to generate plausible but severe macroeconomic narratives and financial shock scenarios, enabling a broader and more granular exploration of potential risks.
  • Model Overlays & Adjustments: Developing and implementing effective model overlays and expert adjustments to address limitations in underlying models or incorporate qualitative insights.
  • Climate Risk & ESG Integration: Integrating emerging risks such as climate change scenarios and broader ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) factors into your stress testing frameworks.
  • Capital Impact & Risk-Weight Simulations: Performing detailed simulations to quantify the impact of stress scenarios on capital adequacy, risk-weighted assets, and key financial metrics.
  • End-to-End Documentation & Governance: Ensuring comprehensive documentation, rigorous model validation, and robust governance for all stress testing processes, ensuring audit-readiness.

AI Enablement: AI models are deployed to project nonlinear impacts of stressors across complex portfolios, providing a more nuanced understanding of risk aggregation. Additionally, AI assists in simulating emerging risk combinations, allowing for the exploration of novel and inter-connected threats that traditional methods might miss.
