Our Services

Model Risk Management

Build robust MRM frameworks, conduct validations, implement model governance, and ensure compliance with global regulatory standards like SR11-7 and ECB TRIM.

AI & Machine Learning

Design and validate AI-driven models, manage algorithmic risk, interpretability testing (XAI), and align with the latest AI Act and model auditability standards.

Stress Testing & Scenario Analysis

Develop macroeconomic stress frameworks, design reverse stress tests, and enhance capital planning using AI-based scenario simulation engines.

Regulatory Advisory

Advisory on PRA, ECB, Fed, Basel IV, and ESG reporting requirements. We assist in supervisory response strategy, regulatory interpretation, and policy reviews.

Data Strategy & Model Inventory

Develop integrated data pipelines, model inventories, metadata tagging, and lifecycle governance using AI-enabled data quality and lineage solutions.

Audit & Remediation

Conduct independent reviews, challenge models post-audit, and design remediation plans with documented traceability, bias mitigation, and risk tiering.
